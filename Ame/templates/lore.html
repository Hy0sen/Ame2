The origin..

Passion is found at a young age they say, everyone has to start somewhere well at the point of me writing this I still have not accompolished much per se..
but still I have passed high school, managed to get in a decent enough college and am still motivated enough to continue pursuing my passion. What entails is not
a very interesting tale but nevertheless it is a memory that I cherish and I will continue to until forever.

Way back in 3rd grade I got gifted a set of encyclopedias, from my cousin. And among that miniature system I found a star which was going to change my life
The book about space that's it, as simple as that. Everything in that book was something that I fell in love with instantly, too young to understand anything but
what pulled me in(literally) were the vivid illustrations of the mysterious black holes, beautiful nebuals, grand galaxies, the marvellous machines that we call rockets, everything.
Ever since, I made it my life's goal to become an astronaut and this single goal of mine has managed to
keep me going no matter what and it is something I am truly grateful for...........
